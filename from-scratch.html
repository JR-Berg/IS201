About me!

Note: This webpage is to demonstrate knowledge of how to use HTML, not to demonstrate a sense of aesthetic aptitude. I am good at making things show up a certain way on a website, I am not good at knowing what a good website looks like.

Some things that are important to know about me:

  1. I'm color blind. Some combinations that are especially challenging to me include:
    • Blue/Purple (impossible for me to tell apart without prior knowledge of what an item's color is)
    • Red/Green
    • Green/Yellow
    • Pink/Grey
    • Dark green or dark red/Brown
    • Dark colors in general are problematic for me
  2. I have severe ADHD
    • I have found that caffeine can help with this, and I am generally fairly good at managing this.
  3. I very much enjoy ironic humor, such as poorly designed webpages.

These things can cause issues if somebody doesn't know them when they are tasked with working alongside me, but with proper communication, I can adapt to fit situations well enough for the most part.
